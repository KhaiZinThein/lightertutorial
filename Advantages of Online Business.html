Advantages of Online Business (Where to apply the skill that taught in tutorial)

You may want to know that the reason of the merchants to adopt changing from physical to online. Is there any advantage? Three of the advantages will be shown as below.

  • Convenient

    • - A working computer can help you with everything

  • Less staff costs

    • - Hire a shop manager, shipping and accounting staff only

  • No time and geographical restriction

    • - No need to worry about customers visiting in an early or late time, or even can't physically visit

When you are considering about the profit of a company, you will figure out that the most of the cost, including operating cost, of an online business is always lower cost than a physical business. You may suggest this to your supervisor or boss about online business and making an app as well.
